HOW IT WORKS

  • 1. CLICK THE "RUN" BUTTON WHEN IT'S YOUR TURN TO GET YOUR CHALLENGE.


  • 2. DO THE "DARE" OR ANSWER THE "TRUTH" TO GET POINTS THAT WILL MAKE YOUR RUNNER RUNS
    AND YOU CAN PICK TO EITHER TAKE A SMALL SIP OF YOUR DRINKS OR SKIP DRINKING.


  • 3. IF YOU REFUSE OR SWERVE AROUND YOUR "TRUTH" ANSWER (ANSWERED WITH A "NON-ANSWER"):
    • - YOU WILL LOSE POINTS (YOUR RUNNER WILL RUN BACKWARDS).
    • - AND YOU HAVE TO TAKE A SHOT OR A BIG GULP OF YOUR DRINK.


  • 4. WINNER: THE FIRST PLAYER TO REACH THE FINISH LINE.
